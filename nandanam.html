PSR NANDANAM

PSR Nandanam is all set to become a contemporary style villament residential housing layout to reside in North Bangalore. The enviable location is just a 10 minute drive to the Kempegowda International airport. It is also surrounded by major residential apartment complexes, and villa projects; PSR Nandanam has top of the line educational institutions within a short distance radius, and also surrounded with nearby world class health care options



Facilites


Intercom

Underground drainage

Electricity & phone lines

Rain water harvesting

Sewage treatment plant

24 Hours security

CCTV surveillance

All round compound wall



Structure

RCC frame work structure
First quality concrete block for walls i.e., Solid blocks with 6” and 4” thickness

Water

Bore - Well water through underground / overhead tanks of suitable capacity will be provided

Kitchen

Raised cooking platform of 20mm thickness with Hassan Green / Block granite slab
Ceramic tiles add up to 4’ above the platform


FLOORING

Vitrified tiles for living, dining, bedrooms and kitchen (800 x 800mm)
Common areas will be provided with ceramic tiles and staircase with 20mm granite
1’ x 1’ anti skid vitrified tiles for balcony and utilities

Bathrooms

Ceramic tiles add up to 7’ height (Johnson / Ceracon - 2’ x 1’ tiles)
Good quality sanitary ware( Hindware / Parryware) and C.P. BRASS FITTING OF JAGUAR / ESS - ESS or equivalent will be provided

Electrical

Concealed electrical wiring with multi strand insulated copper wires
Circuit breakers for safety and suitable points for power and lighting
Switches are of Anchor Roma or equivalent.
